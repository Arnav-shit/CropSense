The CropSense Process

We combine diverse data sources with advanced AI to deliver precise, actionable insights.

1. Data Collection

Multispectral/hyperspectral imagery from drones and satellites, combined with on-ground sensor data, provides a complete picture of every inch of your field.

2. AI Analysis

Our AI models process terabytes of data to detect subtle signs of crop stress, nutrient deficiencies, and pest infestations long before they are visible.

3. Actionable Insights

Receive a clear, simple report on a dashboard, with precise GPS coordinates for problem areas and recommended treatments to optimize your yield.
